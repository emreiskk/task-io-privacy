Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 26, 2025

The TASK.io application is committed to protecting the privacy of its users. This Privacy Policy explains how your personal information is collected, used, and protected when using the TASK.io mobile application.

By using our application, you accept the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

1. INFORMATION COLLECTED

1.1 Account Information

  • Email address
  • Password (stored encrypted)
  • Username and profile information

1.2 Usage Data

  • Tasks and task details you create
  • Task statuses (completed, pending, archived, saved)
  • In-app preferences (theme, language, notification settings)
  • Category and tag preferences

1.3 Technical Data

  • Device information (operating system, app version)
  • App usage statistics
  • Error reports

2. USE OF INFORMATION

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • Service Delivery: Providing task management and calendar features
  • Account Management: Authentication and account security
  • Personalization: Customizing experience according to your theme, language and notification preferences
  • Improvement: Enhancing app performance and user experience
  • Security: Detecting and preventing unauthorized access

3. DATA SECURITY

We take the security of your personal information seriously:

  • All data is protected with secure encryption methods
  • Passwords are stored with secure algorithms and can never be viewed as plain text
  • Only authorized systems can access data
  • Regular security updates are performed
  • Industry-standard security protocols are used

4. DATA SHARING

We do not share your personal information with third parties. Your data may only be shared in the following situations:

  • Legal Requirements: Legal processes or official authority requests
  • Security: Protection against fraud and security threats
  • Your Consent: When you give explicit permission

NEVER: Your tasks and personal data are never sold, rented or shared with third parties for advertising or marketing purposes.

5. USER RIGHTS

You have the following rights over your personal data:

  • Right of Access: Learning what data is stored about you
  • Right of Correction: Correcting and updating your profile information
  • Right of Deletion: Completely deleting your account and all data
  • Portability: Exporting your tasks and data
  • Right to Object: Objecting to data processing activities

You can perform account deletion through 'Settings > Privacy & Security > Delete Account' section.

6. DATA RETENTION PERIOD

  • Data is stored for active accounts until the account is deleted
  • All your data is permanently deleted after account deletion
  • Deletion process is completed within 30 days
  • Except for data that must be retained for legal requirements

7. INTERNATIONAL DATA TRANSFER

Your data may be processed and stored on servers in different countries. During these transfers, appropriate security measures are taken and data protection standards are complied with.

8. PRIVACY POLICY CHANGES

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. When there are significant changes:

  • In-app notification will be sent
  • The 'Last Updated' date at the top of the policy will be updated

Changes take effect from the updated date. We recommend checking regularly.

9. NOTIFICATIONS AND PREFERENCES

  • You can manage your notification settings anytime from 'Settings > Notifications' section
  • You have the right to disable all notifications
  • Your notification preferences are stored locally on your device

10. LEGAL COMPLIANCE

This Privacy Policy has been prepared in accordance with the following regulations:

  • KVKK (Personal Data Protection Law) - Turkey
  • GDPR (General Data Protection Regulation) - European Union
  • CCPA (California Consumer Privacy Act) - United States
